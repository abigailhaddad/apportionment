DHS Spending Lifecycle Tracker

Track funding from Congressional appropriation through obligations to actual outlays

⚠️ IMPORTANT: This tool is still in development. Please contact me before using it for any analysis.

Known Data Limitations:

  • FY 2025 apportionment data is incomplete (OMB MAX stopped reporting mid-year)
  • Data accuracy and completeness are still being validated
About this view: This tool shows how DHS funding flows through the federal spending process:
  • Apportionment: Budget authority allocated by OMB from Congressional appropriations
  • Obligations: Legal commitments to spend (contracts, grants, salaries)
  • Outlays: Actual payments made (cash out the door)

Note: This view shows only current fiscal year appropriations. Federal agencies often spend from multi-year and no-year funds, so total obligations and outlays may include spending from prior year appropriations not shown here.
